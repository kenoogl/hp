abstract: 'ChOWDER is an open-source, web-based scalable display system that consists of multiple display devices on which a web browser operates in cooperation to construct a single large pixel space. Newly introduced functionality of displaying 3D geographic information systems allows us to show large 3D geographic information on ultra-high-resolution tiled display system. This paper describes the method of implementation, use cases, and related works of this functionality.'
